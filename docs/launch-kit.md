## Projects page copy

### Short version

Scribe is a lightweight macOS transcription app built for journalists, writers, researchers, and founders. Drop in a voice memo or interview recording, keep the processing local, and get back clean text fast.

### Medium version

Scribe is the companion app to VoiceType, but it earns its place on its own. It helps you process everything you already recorded: iPhone voice memos, interviews, meetings, and rough field audio. It is designed to stay lightweight, private, and easy to use on macOS.

## Launch notes

- Lead with privacy, speed, and simplicity.
- Frame it as a focused tool, not a bloated productivity suite.
- Mention the sister-app relationship, but do not let it overpower Scribe's own story.
- Use the newsroom angle as the differentiator: rough audio, deadlines, sensitive material, clean copy.
- Keep copy short, memorable, and functional.
